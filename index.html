Q: What is an Affiliate Program?
A: In simple terms and affiliate is a partnership between you (the Affiliate) and us (the Merchant), in which we compensate you with commission for sending users to us via your website, or any other traffic source we permit, where they sign up for OceanBet, and generate revenue.

Q: Why should I work with OceanBet Partners?
A: By promoting the magnificent www.oceanbet.com, which offers your customers sports betting, eSports, casino, slots, live casino, and virtual sports betting, you can make money from your website. Joining is cost-free, and you get to work with not only a great brand, but a great team behind the brand.

Q: How do I sign-up at OceanBet Partners?
A: You can access the affiliate registration process by simply clicking one of the "JOIN NOW" links on our website (www.oceanbetpartners.com). After your account has been verified and authorised, you will be able to login and access your tracking links and marketing materials. At that point, you can add them to your website and begin promoting OceanBet.

Commission

Q: How do I earn money?
A: At OceanBet Partners we are happy to look at all types of commission structures including Revenue Share (where you get paid a % of the net revenue generated by your players), CPA (where you get a fixed amount per real money player), Hybrid (a combination of rev share and CPA), and Fixed Fee (a fixed amount of commission per month). We are also open to look at CPM, CPI deals too.

Q: How to I check my earnings?
A: This is displayed in your OceanBet Partners Affiliate Program account, where you have access to a wealth of reporting options to assess the effectiveness of each campaign and marketing asset in addition to how much money you are presently and have made in the previous months.

Payments

Q: How do I get paid?
A: You must earn more than €100 in a calendar month in order to receive payment. It is imperative as well that you make sure that your payment information, or the payment option you selected when you signed up, is up-to-date in your affiliate account. If your earnings are less than €100, the fee will be carried over to subsequent months until you have earned more.

Q: When will I receive payments?
A: If you earned more than €100 in a calendar month and made sure that your payment information was entered into your affiliate account, you can anticipate getting your payment by the 15th of the following month.

Technical

Q: I do I get my tracking links?
A: You can search through the campaigns in the Affiliate Program to locate your tracking links and banners. Once you've found what you're looking for, you can see the code that needs to be added to your website as well as the links that are currently active.

Q: How do players get tracked to my account?
A: Your unique tracking links created in your account are used to track players, using click ID tracking, which is far superior to cookie-based tracking as 100% of the clicks will be tracked to you. Your tracking link includes tracking parameters that associate the click with your affiliate account. When a player creates a OceanBet account using one of your tracking links, they are permanently associated with your account for all their subsequent activity.

Q: How does the reporting work?
A: In your affiliate account, you can organise the data into different reports so that you can see it whatever format you like. There are many data fields available to you, and everything is very simple. The reporting includes general reporting of clicks, registrations, deposits made by new customers, commissions, and earnings; however, you may also divide the data by player to give you a more granular level if you wish.
